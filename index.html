Call of Duty: Black Ops 7

Releases November 14, 2025

Call of Duty: Black Ops 7 is making a comeback of franchise favorite maps


It was recently announced that fan-favorite map 'Nuketown' will be making a reappearance in the next release for Call of Duty: Black Ops 7 this upcoming November 14th. The game will be released on PlayStation 4 and 5, Xbox One, Xbox series X/S, and PC. The map Nuketown was first released in Call of Duty: Black ops, or the first instalment of the Black Ops series. Many long-term fans are excited to see this map being pulled out of retirement so they can experience some nostalgia with this beloved map.

Halo

Coming in 2026

Halo 1 remake has been announced and coming to PS5


It is finally time for Halo: Campaign Evolved to get its remake; however, fans are having mixed feelings about this anticipated remake. When Microsoft announced the game, it was also announced that Online PVP Multiplayer modes will not be in the game. However, Multiplayer will still be available in campaign co-op. The game will also finally be making its way to the PS5 after many years of rivalry between Microsoft and Sony. The game has a release window until the end of 2026 ans will be available on PlayStation 5, Xbox consoles, and PC.
